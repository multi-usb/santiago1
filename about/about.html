Ing. Santiago Labrador

Hello! I’m Santiago Labrador, a Multimedia Engineer from Bogotá, Colombia.

I blend the precision of a systems engineer, the innovation of an electronics engineer, and the creativity of an audiovisual artist. My expertise spans across a wide range of fields including digital illustration, Design, Web/Mobile Development, cinematography, creative writing, and more.

With a passion for crafting engaging user experiences and visual storytelling, I specialize in creating impactful multimedia solutions. Whether you’re interested in collaborating or just want to chat, feel free to reach out! get in touch.
